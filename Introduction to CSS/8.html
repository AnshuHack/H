About Us

Physics Wallah (PW) is an educational technology company that provides affordable learning experiences. It was founded in 2016 by Alakh Pandey, an educator from Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh. Pandey is the company's co-founder and CEO. PW's goal is to provide high-quality educational content at an affordable price for students preparing for JEE, NEET, NDA, GATE, and UPSC exams. PW takes a student-centric approach by creating customized study plans. Faculties consider the strengths and weaknesses of each student and identify areas that need more attention.
