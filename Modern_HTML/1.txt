Features of HTML5:
1.Multimedia Support: Provides built in support for elements such as video and audio without need for plugin.
2.Form Improvement:It includes new elements and form attributes such as time, date, and color. Required and autofocus in input types of the tag element as well.
3.It has support for local storage, as HTML5 introduced local storage APIs.
4.Geological API: HTML5 introduced geological APIs for obtaining the user's location.
5.Structure of document:It support more semantic element, such as <header>, <footer>, <nav> and <article> etc., thereby Improved accessibility.
