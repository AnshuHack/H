HTML Text formatting

  1. Bold(b)
    • Use to make text darker.
    • PLease avoid to use for long paragraph
    • Do not over use it
  2. italic(i)
    • Used to emphasis a word and used to diffrentiate between word and phrase
    • Can be used for book and Movies title,phrase or foreign words,or to denote thoughts or quotoes etc
    • Avoid overusing bcoz it result in distracting and loose its impact.
  3. underline(u)
    • It is used to underline the word or a sentence.
    • Avoid using to apply for a long sentence as it result in difficult to read.
    • low priority as compared to bold and italic
  4. strong
    • Use to indicate importance or significance of text
    • Can be used to highlight key points, warnings, or notices
    • Can be combined with other formatting tags, such as bold or italic, to create emphasis
  5. strike through(s)
    • It is used to show the given text is deleted by striking it with a horizontal line over the text
